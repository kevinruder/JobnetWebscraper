[A postdoc position in epidemiology and demography is available at the National Institute of Public Health (NIPH), University of Southern Denmark  from June 1, 2018 or as soon as possible. The position is time-limited to 3 years. NIPH is situated in central Copenhagen.
The researcher will lead a project of factors influencing HPV vaccination uptake and unintended consequences with focus on life-style and social factors. The researcher will lead the project in collaboration with a group of researchers. The researcher will also be responsible for teaching in demography and public health at the public health undergraduate programme at the Odense campus.
The researcher is expected to further develop his or her own research profile within the institute, amongst other things via fundraising and scientific publication. In addition, there will be opportunities to contribute more generally to the institutional tasks within research, teaching and supervision.
At the institute, we conduct research within themes relevant for public health of which several studies have a specific focus on adolescent health. The researcher will be part of the epidemiology research group with numerous studies of lifestyle and social factors influencing health.
Qualifications:
•        PhD-degree within epidemiology and public health
•        Documented experience with conducting quantitative research in the field of public health, population health and morbidity and life-style behaviors
•        Experience in analyzing larger data sets – e.g. survey or register data
•        Experience in teaching within public health or similar topics
•        Experience with communicating and collaborating with the general public and public institutions
•        Preferable experience in using statistical programming programmes such as SAS
We can offer an exciting job in an inspiring research environment, with highly motivated colleagues, situated at the heart of Copenhagen.
Further information can be obtained from associate professor Lau Caspar Thygesen at lct@niph.dk.
The successful applicant will be employed in accordance with the agreement between the Ministry of Finance and AC (the Danish Confederation of Professional Associations).
